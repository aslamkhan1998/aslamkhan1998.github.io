App Highlights

Designed with elegance and reliability in mind, bringing you high-quality utility without clutter.

Precise Calculations

Supports major Islamic calculation methods globally (MWL, ISNA, Egypt, Umm al-Qura Makkah, Karachi, Gulf, and more) to ensure exact local prayer timings wherever you are.

Beautiful Azan Alarms

Choose from multiple beautiful recorded Azan audio files (including Makkah, Madinah, Al-Aqsa, and shorter alert sound variants) to play automatically at prayer times, even in the background.

Home Screen Widgets

View upcoming prayer countdowns and the day's schedule directly from your device home screen. Clean, minimal widgets that support light, dark, and system matching modes.

100% Privacy Focused

Your coordinates and settings never leave your device. Calculations are computed offline locally. The app does not collect personal data or track location in the background.
