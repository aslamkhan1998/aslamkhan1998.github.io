Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. This privacy policy document details the types of information that is collected and recorded by Azan Times World (the "Application" or "App") and how we use it.

1. Overview & Data Minimization

Azan Times World is built as an offline-first utility application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal details, calculations, or locations to our own remote servers. Everything needed to compute prayer times and play Azan alarms is processed locally on your device.

2. Location Information

The Application requires access to your device's geographical coordinates (latitude, longitude) to calculate precise Islamic prayer times for your current location.

  • How it is used: The coordinates are used strictly on-device inside the mathematical calculation engine.
  • No Uploading: Location data is never uploaded to any remote server or shared with third parties. It is stored securely in the app's local memory or Expo SecureStore.
  • Manual Option: If you do not wish to grant location permissions, you can choose to enter coordinates manually or search for your city within the app.

3. Audio and Background Tasks

To notify you at exact prayer times, the App schedules notifications and audio playback using Android/iOS background systems. The audio files (Azan recordings) are bundled directly inside the app package and are played locally. No streaming is done, and no user audio is recorded or stored.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep this app free, we display advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK).

Google AdMob may collect and process information about your use of the Application:

  • Device Identifiers: AdMob uses unique device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, track ad engagement, and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Device Information: General parameters like device type, screen size, operating system version, and system language may be processed to optimize ad rendering.
  • Privacy Preferences: You can manage or reset your advertising identifier and limit personalized tracking directly through your device's operating system settings (e.g., Google Settings -> Ads or Apple Settings -> Privacy).

For more information on how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services, please visit: Google Partner Sites Policy.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your preferences. Since all information (such as prayer time settings, selected azan sounds, and coordinate offsets) is kept in your device's local memory, it benefits from the high security standards of your device's operating system.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
